Pancake Recipe

Fluffy, Golden, and Delicious

Ever crave a sweet and satisfying breakfast treat?
Something easy to make and fun to eat?
Look no further than this Ultimate Pancake Recipe!
Keep reading to know how you can achieve this breakfast masterpiece.


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 2 tablespoons sugar

  • 2 teaspoons baking powder

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • 1 cup milk

  • 1 large egg

  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

  • Optional: blueberries, chocolate chips, or fruit for added flavor

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.

  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. The batter should still be slightly lumpy.

  4. If desired, add blueberries, chocolate chips, or fruit to the batter and fold them in.

  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Drop the batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto the skillet or griddle.

  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges start to dry. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until golden brown.

  7. Serve the pancakes hot with your favorite toppings, such as syrup, butter, or fresh fruit. Enjoy!
